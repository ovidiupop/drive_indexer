Folders

In the Folders tab happen the magic.

Here you have to select the folders which you wish to index. Click on Add to add new Folder. After you select a folder from your File Manager, this will be added to the list and the indexing will automatically begin.

If you selected to index a very large folder, there will be a lag of some seconds until will start. You will know that the indexer is working as long as the buttons are disabled.

While the files are indexed, you will see the progress in the status bar (and in the information area under the list).

If you decide to remove some folders, the indexed content of them will be removed from database, and you will not find anymore any references to them.
You can remove more folders at once. Select those folders and click on Remove.
If you wish to completely clean your database, you can use Remove All button. This will remove all folders and the files indexed.

If for any reason you wish to reindex one or more folders, you have to select them and then click on Re/Index button. Previous records will be removed and the folders will be reindex.

During the indexation all operations which can affect the result will be blocked. You will not be able to add/remove folders, and also you will not be able to remove/add extensions.
