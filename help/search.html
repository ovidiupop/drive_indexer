Search

Search tab contain the main page, because here you can find your desired files and this was the reason of the application!

If you already indexed your folders, you have to insert your term for search, press Enter or click on the Search button and the results' table will be filled with results.

If you wish to search only for a specific category of files, you can uncheck the rest of categories.

You can sort results as you wish, clicking on the table's header.

If some of the results belong to a drive which is not mounted, the name of that drive will be red.

If the results belong to a mounted drive, you can see very fast information about each file with a double click on the line of table. Many files have also preview.
